About Us!

Hi, Dearest Pastry Lovers! Chocochu Bakery since 2019 are coming for you! Yippey!

The Chococu Bakery is Indonesian leading bakery that specializes in cakes and pastry. The company is owned and manage by Sekar Andini Group. The first Chococu opened on December 7, 2019. And now, Chococu had 29 outlets in Indonesia and 21 in Korea.

All of our products are served fresh from the oven! So you'll get exclusive taste that obviously delicious! Oh, don't forget to visit our first outlet in Tangerang, Indonesia.
